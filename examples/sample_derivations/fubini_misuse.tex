\begin{proof}
Let $f_n \to f$ pointwise and suppose $f_n \geq 0$.
By Fubini, we swap the order of integration:
\[
  \int_\Omega \int_X f_n \, d\mu \, d\mathbb{P}
  = \int_X \int_\Omega f_n \, d\mathbb{P} \, d\mu.
\]
It clearly follows that
\[
  \lim_{n \to \infty} \int_X f_n \, d\mu = \int_X f \, d\mu,
\]
since obviously $f_n$ converges to $f$ and the dominated convergence theorem applies.
We conclude $\int_X f \, d\mu < \infty$.
\end{proof}
